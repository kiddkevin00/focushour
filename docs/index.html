Focus
Hour.

A quiet Pomodoro timer for iPhone. Twenty-five minutes of work, five of rest — no tracking, no streak guilt, no upsells.

v1.0.0·iPhone · iOS 16+

01

Overview

How it works

Tap Start. The dial winds down twenty-five minutes by default — when it completes you get a haptic tap, a local notification, and the next mode (a five-minute break) cues up automatically.

  • Pause, reset, or skip at any time.
  • Today's count and minutes focused live below the dial.
  • The screen stays awake while a session is running.
  • A local notification fires when the session ends, even if you backgrounded the app.
02

Customize

Can I change the lengths?

Yes — tap Settings in the top right. Focus length and break length are independent, 1 to 120 / 1 to 60 minutes respectively. The classic Pomodoro ratio is 25 / 5; a longer 50 / 10 is popular for deep work.

03

Notifications

Why does the app ask for notification permission?

So the end-of-session alert fires even when the app is backgrounded or your phone is locked. If you decline, the dial still counts down and you'll see the completion in-app — you just won't get a banner.

04

Data

Is there a history?

Yes — the last 200 completed sessions are kept on-device to compute today's stats. They are never uploaded. You can wipe them by deleting and reinstalling the app.
