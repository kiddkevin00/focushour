Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Why does the app ask for notification permission?

So the end-of-session alert fires even when the app is backgrounded or your phone is locked. If you decline, the dial still counts down and you'll see the completion in-app — you just won't get a banner.

Is there a leaderboard or streak counter?

No. Today's stats are visible in-app and that's it. No streak shaming, no friends-list comparison, no analytics, no sharing prompts.

Can I change the lengths?

Yes — tap Settings. Focus and break are independent. The classic Pomodoro ratio is 25/5; 50/10 is popular for deeper work.

Does the timer keep running in the background?

Yes, in the sense that the end-time is locked at start. The dial may visually freeze while backgrounded, but the notification still fires on time and the state reconciles when you reopen the app.
