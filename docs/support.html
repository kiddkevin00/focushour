Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Does the timer keep running when I lock my phone?

Yes. The dial is wall-clock under the hood: the end time is computed when you tap Start, and the dial reconciles itself against the system clock on every tick and whenever the app comes back to the foreground.

Will the notification still fire if the app is backgrounded?

Yes — it's a local notification scheduled by iOS at session start. It will fire even if Focus Hour was killed by the OS while you were away.

Can I change the focus and break lengths?

Yes — tap Settings in the top right. Focus is 1–120 minutes, break is 1–60. The classic ratio is 25/5; 50/10 is popular for deep work.

What does Skip do?

Ends the current session immediately and switches to the other mode (focus → break or vice versa). Does NOT record a completed session.

Does the app collect anything?

No. Only your session log (mode, duration, completion timestamp) and settings live on this device, in AsyncStorage. There are no analytics, no third-party SDKs, no network calls of any kind.
